Essential Metrics to Empower Your Department

Permit Processing Time

Gain insights into how long it takes to process building permits, from application submission to approval. Identify bottlenecks and optimize workflows for quicker turnaround

Permit Issuance Rate

Measure success by calculating the percentage of permit applications that receive approvals. Enhance transparency and accountability within your department

Code Compliance Rate

Ensure safer constructions by tracking the percentage of projects that meet building codes and regulations. Prioritize adherence to quality standards

Revenue Generation

Monitor the financial health of your department by keeping tabs on revenue from permit related services. Make informed decisions to support sustainable growth.

Plan Review Cycle Time

Efficiently manage plan reviews by assessing the time it takes for constructive feedback. Enhance collaboration and reduce delays

Resource Utilization

Optimize your resources, from staff capacity to inspection allocation. Maximize efficiency while maintaining quality control

Permit Application Types

Understand the demand landscape with insights into the distribution of permit applications by type. Plan for future projects more effectively.

Staff Productivity

Measure staff productivity by tracking permits processed or inspections conducted per staff member. Foster a culture of continuous improvement
